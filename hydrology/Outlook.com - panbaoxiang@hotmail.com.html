                    Cancellation Policy
                    Written notification of registration cancellations must be e-mailed to agu@experient-inc.com. Registration cancellations received by 14 November 2014 will receive a complete refund. Cancellations received between 15  and 21 November 2014 will have a $50 processing fee deducted from their refund. No refunds will be issued for registrations or ticketed events cancelled after 21 November 2014.
